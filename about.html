About Permits & More

Your trusted partner for exceptional engineering and permit services throughout Southern California.

Permits & More provides exceptional engineering and permit services for a wide range of projects. We specialize in guiding projects seamlessly from the conceptual phase through design and into permitting with city building departments—delivering accuracy, efficiency, and timelines you can rely on.

Our mission is to simplify what is often a long and complex permitting process. Serving the City of San Diego, the County of San Diego, and surrounding areas, we help make obtaining building permits faster, easier, and far less stressful for our clients. For over five years, we've supported architects, developers, sign contractors, general contractors, and homeowners with dependable engineering and permit processing services.

In addition, we offer building code violation assistance, helping clients resolve violations and maintain full code compliance. Our goal is to protect your investment and help you maximize the value of your property with confidence.
